Why Bhadrak BEAUTIFUL ?

Bhadrak city is strategically located in the eastern part of the Indian state of Odisha. It lies on the banks of the River Salandi and serves as a gateway to the northern districts of Odisha.

Bhadrak is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and vibrant culture. Some of the reasons that make Bhadrak more attractive include its stunning temples, picturesque landscapes, delicious cuisine, and friendly locals. Additionally, Bhadrak boasts a thriving tourism industry, with many visitors coming to explore its historical and cultural sites. Overall, the city's many charms make it a popular destination for travelers.

Our Bhadrak

The district has its administrative center at Bhadrak, which is also known as Dikbali in Oriya. Dikbali is known for its mix of Indo-Aryan and Oriya cultures. Bhadrak is home to many historic sites, including the Jaina Digambara Tirthankara temple and the Radhanagar Temple. The city is also known for its traditional handicrafts, such as weaving and woodcarving.
